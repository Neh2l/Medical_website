Our medical specalists

Neurology

Neurology is the branch of medicine that deals with disorders of the brain, spinal cord, and nervous system. It includes conditions like epilepsy, stroke, and migraines.

Dentistry

Dentistry involves the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of oral diseases. It focuses on teeth, gums, and overall oral health.

Infectious Diseases

This specialty deals with infections caused by viruses, bacteria, and other microorganisms. It includes diseases like COVID-19, influenza, and hepatitis.

Cardiology

Cardiology is the study and treatment of heart and blood vessel conditions like heart attacks, hypertension, and arrhythmias

Emergency Medicine

Emergency medicine provides immediate care for acute illnesses, injuries, and life-threatening conditions such as accidents, strokes, and heart attacks.

Ophthalmology

Ophthalmology focuses on eye health, including vision problems, eye diseases, and surgical procedures like cataract removal and laser correction.
